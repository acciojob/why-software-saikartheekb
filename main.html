Why I want to become a developer?

I have a passion for technology and a desire to create useful and innovative programs. Many software developers also enjoy problem-solving and the process of turning an idea into a functioning piece of software. Additionally, the field of software development is constantly evolving, providing developers with the opportunity to continuously learn new skills and stay up-to-date with the latest technologies. And also the potential for high salary and good job opportunity are some other motivations for people to become software developer.
